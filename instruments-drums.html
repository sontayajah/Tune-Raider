Drums

Press the computer keyboard to play a sound.

Timer:
Y E R C S D G H X J U
You can use the following shortcuts:
  • Crash cymbal: y
  • Ride cymbal: u
  • Hi-hat (closed): r
  • Hi-hat (open): e
  • Hi-hat (foot): c
  • High tom-tom: g
  • Low tom-tom: h
  • Floor tom: j
  • Snare drum: s
  • Snare drum (cross stick): d
  • Bass drum: x
